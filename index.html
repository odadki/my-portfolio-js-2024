About

In 2021 I stumbled across a web development bootcamp ad. I knew nothing of coding, but was intrigued by the initial course and found myself diving deep down the rabbit hole of frontend web development and UX/UI design. Soon after, I was lucky to land a web development internship at Push10, a design agency.

Currently, I am a web developer at Loyola Law School, assisting fellow staff, faculty, students and clinics with their department’s web presence needs. This includes anywhere from maintenance, to troubleshooting, to building new websites in Drupal, WordPress, and TerminalFour.

At the end of the day, I appreciate an attractive, well-built site, and use that as my motto for building sites at work and for my own personal projects.
