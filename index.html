About

Hi, I'm Danielle

In 2021, I came across a web development bootcamp ad with no prior coding experience, but I was intrigued. What started as curiosity quickly turned into a deep dive into frontend development and UX/UI design. Shortly after, I was fortunate to secure a web development internship at Push10, a design agency.

I currently work as a web developer at LMU Loyola Law School, supporting staff, faculty, students, and clinics with their web presence—from site maintenance and troubleshooting to building new websites using Drupal, WordPress, and TerminalFour.

At the core of my work is a simple motto: build attractive, well-crafted websites. Whether it’s for my job or personal projects, that’s the standard I strive for every day.
